Crystal Collector

Game Instructions

Welcome to the Crystal Collector game! There are four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. * You will be shown a random number at the start of the game. * When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. * You won't know this amount until you click a crystal. * When you do click one, it will update the player's score counter. * You wins if the total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. * You lose if their score goes above the random number.

The game restarts again with a new random number whenever you win or lose. Also, all the crystals will have four new hidden values.
